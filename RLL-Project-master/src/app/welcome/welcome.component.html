Inventory Management

An inventory management system is the process by which you track your goods throughout your entire supply chain, from purchasing to production to end sales.It governs how you approach inventory management for your business.It basically refers to the process of ordering, storing, using, and selling a company's inventory. This includes the management of raw materials, components, and finished products, as well as warehousing and processing of such items.The primary purpose of inventory management is to ensure there is enough goods or materials to meet demand without creating overstock, or excess inventory.
